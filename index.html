About

Homesteading is a movement to improve one's personal self sufficiency. This spirit of individuality can be improved upon with a community of knowledge. The presentation and storage of this knowledge can be iterated on like open source software. The Open Source Homestead's goal is to provide instructions to build the products one uses in day to day life and allow others to contribute to this body of knowledge.

In addition it's important to back claims with peer-reviewed science rather than anecdotal observations. Therefore, where possible we will provide scientific references.

The Material published here is shared under a Creative Commons license, which allows commercial use, but requires attribution. This is to allow artisans and makers to use this knowledge in their businesses.
